Laundry SOP

Washing Instructions

Follow these steps to ensure proper washing:

  1. Separate clothes by color: whites, colors, darks.
  2. Use cold water for delicate fabrics.
  3. Add detergent according to load size.
  4. Select appropriate wash cycle (normal, gentle, etc.).

Drying Instructions

Ensure proper drying to avoid damage:

  1. Check garment labels for drying instructions.
  2. Avoid overloading the dryer.
  3. Use low heat for delicate items.
  4. Remove clothes promptly to prevent wrinkles.

Ironing Instructions

Iron clothes carefully to avoid burns:

  1. Set iron to the appropriate temperature for the fabric.
  2. Iron inside-out for delicate fabrics.
  3. Use steam for cotton and linen fabrics.
  4. Hang or fold clothes immediately after ironing.
